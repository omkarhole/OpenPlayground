WCAG Quick Reference

AA — Normal Text

≥ 4.5 : 1

Body text smaller than 18pt (24px) or 14pt bold (18.5px bold).

AA — Large Text

≥ 3 : 1

Text at least 18pt (24px) or 14pt bold (18.5px bold).

AAA — Normal Text

≥ 7 : 1

Enhanced contrast for maximum readability.

AAA — Large Text

≥ 4.5 : 1

Enhanced contrast for large text elements.
