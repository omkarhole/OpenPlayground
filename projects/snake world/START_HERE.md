## ğŸ¤” FAQ

**Q: Do I need to install anything to play?**
A: No! Just open `index.html`. For multiplayer, you need Node.js.

**Q: Why is the game so slow?**
A: Slow = easier to control and think ahead. Perfect for beginners!

**Q: How do I get more points?**

A: Game ends and shows your final stats.
