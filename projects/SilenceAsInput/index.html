How SilenceAsInput Works

  • Be Still: Don't move your mouse, don't type, don't scroll
  • Build Progress: Stillness accumulates progress over time
  • Avoid Noise: Any interaction resets your progress
  • Resistance Matters: Each reset tests your impulse resistance
  • Patience Wins: The longer you remain still, the higher your score

This is an experimental design that measures restraint, not action.
