How to Play

This is a memory matching game with emotional faces. The goal is to find all matching pairs of emotions.

  • Click on a card to flip it and reveal an emotion
  • Click on another card to find its matching pair
  • If the two cards match, they will remain face-up
  • If they don't match, they will flip back over
  • Complete the game by finding all 8 pairs
